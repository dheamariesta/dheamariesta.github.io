Other Projects

Starlight, 2018

Showcased at iLight Marina Bay, March 2018.
A light installation to raise awareness of light pollution in Singapore. A collaboration between NTU School of Art, Design and Media and School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (Information Engineering and Media Programme). The structure was divided into 4 sections, with each section having its own sensors that will trigger the desired effect of the starlight, so as to encourage communal effort in making the starlight effect.

Photo credit: Jeremy Ng Cheong Wong

Animal Chess, 2017

The vision of our project is “Bridging The Gap Between Generations With Technology”. We decided to modify the original Animal Chess as a solution to bridge the age gap since it was a well-known and popular board game in the past. By recreating the game, we aim to bring back a sense of nostalgia from the older generation and reel in the younger generation as well.
Features: Interactive light sensors, controllable count down LED and music player using Android App
